Go through the entire tutorial before commencing.
Requisites:
  • XXKPE
  • XXKPQ/XXKPU
  • CF-Root/Ultra Chaos Kernel v15
  • Poseidon Rom
  • Odin v1.85
Note:
  • The process involves flashing XXKPE followed by flashing XXKPQ or XXKPU. Both processes are explained below.
  • XXKPE will convert any file system you have to rfs.
  • XXKPU/XXKPQ and XXKPE both need to be extracted to some desired location.
  • CF-Root and Poseidon ROM need not be extracted.
  • Put the Poseidon zipped file in your external SD card.
  • Wipe cache and factory reset by going to recovery mode (Home button + Volume Up + Power button) after flashing each XXKPE, XXKPU/XXKPQ, CF-Root and Poseidon otherwise you may face random reboots automatically.
  • You might want to give UC kernel a try for extra tweaks and improving the performance.
Set-Up before starting:
  1. Make sure the phone drivers are installed on your PC and Kies is not running.
  2. Backup your apps and any important data.
  3. Take a backup of contacts, by go to Contacts -> Menu -> Import/Export.
  4. Take a backup of SMS via SMS Backup and Restore by Ritesh Sahu.
  5. Copy contact generated vsf file and sms backup file to your PC or laptop.
  6. Keep the usb cable at hand before starting.
  7. Make sure your phone has more than 60% battery.
  8. Switch Off your mobile.
Process:


Re-partitioning
  1. Put your mobile in Downloading mode by Pressing Volume Down key + the Home key and holding them press the Power button.
  2. Now Start ODIN.
  3. Connect Mobile To Computer, you will see the ID:COM port light up in ODIN with port number on which your phone is connected.
  4. Now click on PIT button and select latona_20110114.pit and check the Re-partition below the ID:COM and click on Start button.
  5. It will show a blank screen once it’s flashed, now disconnect the phone from computer and remove your mobile battery and put it back.
Flashing with XXKPE
  1. Put your mobile in Downloading mode by Pressing Volume Down key + the Home key and holding them press the Power button.
  2. Now Start ODIN.
  3. Connect Mobile To Computer, you will see the ID:COM port light up in ODIN with port number on which your phone is connected.
  4. Make sure the repartition box is unchecked.
  5. Now click on BOOT LOADER and select the APBOOT_I9003XXKPE_CL409735_REV08_user_low_ship.tar.md5 file.
  6. Click on PDA and select the CODE_I9003XXKPE_CL409735_REV08_user_low_ship.tar.md5.
  7. Click on PHONE and select the MODEM_I9003XXKPE_REV_00_CL1041909.tar.md5.
  8. Click on CSC and select the GT-I9003-CSC-MULTI-OXAKPE.tar.md.
  9. Now click on Start.
  10. Wait for process to complete and do not detach phone from PC until it finishes successfully and you see ‘PASS’ with green color.
  11. The phone will boot and your firmware now would be 2.3.4 XXKPE.
  12. Turn off the phone.
Flashing with XXKPQ
Flash mobile again with XXKPE. When using XXKPQ it is crucial that you flash XXKPE twice. After flashing with XXKPE twice,
  1. Put your mobile in Downloading mode by Pressing Volume Down key + the Home key and holding them Power button.
  2. Now connect your phone to computer, in ODIN, ID:COM box will turn into yellow.
  3. Click on PDA and select I9003XXKPQ_I9003XXKPQ_I9003SERKPQ_HOME.tar.md5.
  4. Click on start button.
  5. Wait for process to complete and do not detach phone from PC until it finishes successfully and you see ‘PASS’ with green color.
  6. The phone will boot and your firmware now would be 2.3.6 XXKPQ.
  7. Turn off the phone.
Flashing with XXKPU
Skip this if you are using XXKPQ
  1. Put your mobile in Downloading mode by Pressing Volume Down key + the Home key and holding them Power button.
  2. Now connect your phone to computer, in ODIN, ID:COM box will turn into yellow.
  3. Click on PDA and select I9003XXKPU_I9003XXKPU_I9003SERKPU_HOME.tar.md5.
  4. Click on start button.
  5. Wait for process to complete and do not detach phone from PC until it finishes successfully and you see ‘PASS’ with green color.
  6. The phone will boot and your firmware now would be 2.3.6 XXKPU.
  7. Switch off the phone.
Flashing Root
  1. Put your mobile in Downloading mode by Pressing Volume Down key + the Home key and holding them Press the Power button.
  2. Connect you mobile to computer, now click on PDA in ODIN and go to folder where you have downloaded the CF-Root/UC Kernel v15.
  3. Click on start and wait till process got completed.
Flashing the Poseidon ROM
  1. Copy Poseidon file in your external sdcard POSEIDON’S_ROM_XXKPQ_01-09-12.zip.
  2. Switch of your phone and start in recovery mode by Pressing Volume UP key + the Home key and holding them press the Power button.
  3. It will start in CWM recovery mode. Click on install zip from sdcard and just flash the Poseidon zip.
  4. Your phone will auto restart. If not then select reboot phone.
Enjoy Poseidon on your phone!!!
