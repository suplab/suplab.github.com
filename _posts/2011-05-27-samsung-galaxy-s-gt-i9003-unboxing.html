After months of waiting and searching for that perfect device, I had narrowed down my search to Samsung Galaxy S I9003 and I9000. So today decided to finally buy one. Went to buy the I9003 one but ended up buying Samsung Galaxy S GT-I9003. Samsung Galaxy S I9003 can be referred as successor to the all famous Samsung Galaxy S. The only major difference is 1 Ghz TI OMAP 3630 processor, instead of the Hummingbird processor in Galaxy S. Samsung Galaxy S GT-I9003 is branded for India as Galaxy S LCD 4GB but elsewhere it is available as Galaxy SL (16GB version). Personally, I would call it a toned down substitute of Galaxy S I9000. Currently, there's a scarcity of Galaxy S I9000 handsets and the shortage of Super AMOLED displays has been cited as the main reason behind this. Obviously, Samsung has moved on to the new Super AMOLED Plus technology that is better and has used it in upcoming handset - Samsung Galaxy S II GT-I9100.
