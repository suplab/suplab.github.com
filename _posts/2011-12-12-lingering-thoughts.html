This is not a love poem
Addressed to you
Nor a praise of your attributes.
This is the gloomy way
Through which I travel,
Dreaming or awake.
And you haunt my every moment
Invading
But never responding.
Yet I walk on alone, ever alone.
Thrust in this void chasm which,
I can neither cross nor fathom.
I swim solitary
Seeking a ray of hope
That only your touch can provide.
